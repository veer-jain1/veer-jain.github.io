Research

Design and Numerical Study of Origami-Inspired Structures for Axial Compression and Energy Absorption in Crashworthiness Applications

This research investigates origami-inspired geometries such as Miura-ori and Kresling patterns for crashworthiness applications. Parametric CAD models were developed using Rhino and Grasshopper, followed by finite element simulations in ANSYS to analyse deformation behaviour, stress distribution, and energy absorption under axial compression.
